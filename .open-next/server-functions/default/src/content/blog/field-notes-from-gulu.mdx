Community psychology begins with listening—but listening is a *skill*, not an instinct.

## Translation is analysis

When we moved from English to Acholi and back, we built in a “reflective pause” after each prompt. Facilitators could flag when a question landed wrong *before* we locked the guide.

## Compensation matters

We budgeted for youth peer facilitators as co-investigators, not volunteers. The quality of follow-up questions doubled in the second week.

## What we changed mid-stream

1. Shortened cognitive batteries after school days ran long  
2. Added caregiver opt-in SMS summaries (plain language)  
3. Shifted focus groups from classrooms to familiar parish halls

The dataset is richer because the process honored constraints participants already carried.
