Hybrid mentoring fails when it becomes “everything is flexible,” which really means “everything spills into evenings.”

## Anchor the week

We protect a shared ninety-minute block for deep methods conversation. Async comments happen around it, not instead of it.

## Writing in public (within the lab)

Shared folders with dated drafts train students to see scholarship as iteration. I comment drafts in two passes: *structure* first, *polish* later.

## Feedback that names the next step

Instead of “clarify the theory section,” I ask for “three sentences that connect Figure 2 to the mechanism in paragraph 2.” Specificity reduces paralysis.

The goal is momentum: small wins visible on the calendar.
